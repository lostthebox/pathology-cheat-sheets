KEY CONCEPTS

CRITICAL IHC PANELS

  • Adrenal Cortex: SF-1 (nuclear, specific!), inhibin-α, Melan-A (NOT specific to melanoma!), synaptophysin (variable)
  • Pheochromocytoma: Chromogranin, synaptophysin, GATA3, tyrosine hydroxylase (specific), SDHB (loss = SDHx mutation)
  • Neuroblastoma: Synaptophysin, chromogranin, NSE, NB84, TH; S-100 for Schwann cells

RETICULIN PATTERNS (CRITICAL!)

  • Adenoma: Intact reticulin framework maintaining normal acinar architecture
  • Carcinoma: Complete disruption OR pericellular reticulin distribution
  • Weiss Criteria: ≥3 = carcinoma (includes necrosis, mitoses, vascular invasion)

NEUROBLASTIC TUMOR SPECTRUM

  • Least → Most Differentiated: NB → GNB → GN
  • GNB Subtypes (INPC): Nodular (classic/atypical) = UNFAVORABLE; Intermixed = FAVORABLE
  • NB Poor Prognosis: MYCN amplification, 1p deletion, 11q loss, 17q gain, older age, high MKI (Mitosis-Karyorrhexis Index)

SIZE DISTINCTIONS

  • Adrenal Medullary Hyperplasia vs Pheo: <1cm = hyperplasia, >1cm = pheochromocytoma
  • PPNAD vs AIMAH: PPNAD micronodules <1cm; AIMAH macronodules >1cm
  • Adenoma vs Carcinoma: Size alone insufficient; use Weiss criteria + reticulin

HEREDITARY SYNDROMES

  • MEN2A: Medullary thyroid CA + Pheo + Parathyroid hyperplasia (RET mutations)
  • Pheo Hereditary (~50%): VHL, RET/MEN2, NF1, SDHx (SDHB IHC loss screens SDHx)
  • ACC: Li-Fraumeni (TP53), Beckwith-Wiedemann, MEN1, Lynch
  • PPNAD: Carney complex (PRKAR1A)

HIGH-YIELD MORPHOLOGY

  • Zellballen pattern: Pheochromocytoma (nests with sustentacular cells)
  • Homer-Wright rosettes: Neuroblastoma (50%)
  • Neuropil: Neuroblastic tumors (fibrillary background)
  • Pigment: PPNAD (lipofuscin), Melanoma (melanin), GN (neuromelanin)
